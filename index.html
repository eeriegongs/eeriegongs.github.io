About me:

I am a PhD student in the applied math and computational sciences (AMCS) program at the University of Iowa, and a research assistant in the Computational Biomedical Imaging Group (CBIG) led by my PhD advisor Prof. Mathews Jacob.

Current CV (Updated 4/21/2016)

Research interests:

Designing novel image priors for inverse problems in medical imaging, with specific focus on compressed sensing MRI reconstruction. I have published work on:

  • Off-the-grid signal and image recovery.
  • Finite-rate-of-innovation models.
  • Co-sparse analysis models.
  • Generalizations of total variation regularization.
  • Non-convex approaches to large scale sparsity regularized optimization problems.
